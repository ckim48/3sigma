    The Primate Portal Internship Program

    A joint project with Carnegie Mellon University and Rochester University of Technology to find the origin of thoughts. The Primate Portal research lab works with Seneca Park Zoo in Rochester, New York, making use of touchscreen devices and CCTV to study and record baboons' behavior.

    Research related data and raw recorded materials produced by the lab is free and accessible through their official website.

    2022 Summer 3Sigma Internship Program

    Students at this 3Sigma Internship Program provided: data visualizations, data analysis tools, software and hardware development for The Primate Portal to support and improve analysis conducted by researchers at the lab. Additionally, to facilitate the study in understanding animal minds, we implemented robotics, gamified tasks, and software features to study animal cognitive processes.
